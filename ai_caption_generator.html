A.I. Social Media Caption Generator

Generate captions and hashtags for social media posts using this A.I. tool.

This application utilizes a pre-trained model to generate captions for your uploaded images. Please be aware that the model is currently running on a free CPU server, which may result in a brief delay in caption generation. To enhance inference speed, I have implemented quantization techniques.

Having trouble accessing the application?

The application is hosted on HuggingFace Spaces, a platform for sharing machine learning models. I've observed that this website is often blocked on corporate networks that restrict access to artificial intelligence resources. If you're experiencing difficulties accessing the application, try using a personal device such as a smartphone. If the issue persists, please feel free to contact me for assistance.
